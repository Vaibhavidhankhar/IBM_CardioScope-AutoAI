🔍 Project Overview

CardioScope is a machine learning model designed to predict the risk of heart attacks using patient health data. It leverages IBM Watson AutoAI to automatically perform data preprocessing, feature selection, model selection, and hyperparameter tuning — all within a few clicks and lines of Python.

💡 Why It Matters

Cardiovascular diseases cause over 17 million deaths annually. Early detection is crucial. With AI-powered predictions, doctors can proactively treat high-risk patients, reducing mortality and improving health outcomes.
